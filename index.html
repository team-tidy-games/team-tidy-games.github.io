Saucer Sling - GGJ18

Our submission for Global Game Jam 2018: Theme - Transmission.

Roast Royale - GGJ19

Commute Crash

Our Brains Eden 2018 submission: Theme - Unreliable.

A 3D pain simulator game. You're late to work. Again. Your only chance to not get fired is to have a REALLY good excuse. Get as hurt as possible before you go to work.

The Turing Test

A 2D stealth game - Created in C++ using ASGE

A game created as part of an assignment, The Turing Test is about sneaking through a factory and convincing guards that you're a human. Our ambition for this game outgrew our time constraints and so many of the original features didn't make it into the game. Overall a fun slice of our concept.
